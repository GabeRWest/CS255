# CS255
DriverPass Project for CS255
DriverPass, is a client who wants to break into the drivers education space by
provide both online and in-person driving lessons and exams. They wanted me to design
a web-based system in which customers could take practice tests and book appointments
with driving instructors in order to pass their DMV drivers test.

I think I did a great job designing the Activity Diagrams. They were very
straight forward and simple to follow

IF I had to redo any of my work with this project, I would go back and revise the
use case diagram. This is because the lines overlapped and it was all in all just 
difficult to follow.

In order to interpret and implement the needs of my client, I needed to interview them
and ask them many questions. After re-reading the transcript, I was able to piece together a BRD.
Considering the needs of the client is of utmost importance because ultimately, they are the ones who
will be affected by the design decisions made.

As of right now, my strategy for designing software is more or less to build it as I go. In the future,
I think creating BRD's and activity diagrams could be very helpful for guiding my development of future
projects.
